adding fractions

now, let's add fractions, but, with a twist. This time, there will be different BOTTOM numbers and you will need to add them. To do this, you will need to find the LCD (least common denominator), the LCD is the smallest number you can multiply something to get from both denominators. Then, multiply the top and bottom numbers (so the fraction stays the same) to make the bottom numbers the same. Finally, you add. (don't simpilify)
